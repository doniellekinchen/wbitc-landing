About

🧭 What is We Be In The City? ›

We Be In The City (WBITC) is a social vibe curation platform that helps people explore, savor, and fall in love with their city again. Making plans can be overwhelming; between budgets, groups, and vibes, the fun often gets lost in the planning.


WBITC simplifies the process by asking one key question: What’s the vibe? Whether it’s a date, family outing, or night with friends, we surface curated recommendations that fit your mood and budget.


A love letter to Atlanta, WBITC celebrates the city’s culture, diversity, and local gems. From hidden spots to family-friendly adventures, our curations center accessibility, affordability, and inclusivity. Always ATL, always authentic.


What’s next? We’re starting in Atlanta, and as we grow, we’ll expand to new cities, bringing the same vibe and community-powered curations to more places.
